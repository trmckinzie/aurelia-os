Mission Parameters

AURELIA is an Externalized Cortex. It is a cybernetic extension of the biological mind, designed to solve the limits of human memory and processing power. It operates on the principle that knowledge is not static data to be stored, but living "assets" that must be recursively analyzed and evolved.

System Definitions

  • [A]
    AUGMENTED: The use of digital prosthetics (AI, Databases, Search Algorithms) to extend biological cognitive limits.
  • [U]
    UNDERSTANDING: The shift from raw "Data" to synthesized "Wisdom" by linking disparate concepts into a causal network.
  • [R]
    RECURSIVE: The logic of loops. The output of one learning cycle (a note) becomes the input for the next, creating compound interest on knowledge.
  • [E]
    EVOLUTIONARY: The application of Darwinian selection to ideas. Only the strongest arguments ("Cranes") survive; weak ones are discarded.
  • [L]
    LOGIC: The rigorous operating system of the mind. Validity is prioritized over intuition.
  • [I]
    INTELLIGENT: The integration of active agents (LLMs) to retrieve and process information dynamically.
  • [A]
    ANALYSIS: The atomic deconstruction of complex systems into their fundamental "bricks" (First Principles).
